King Gizzard & The Lizard Wizard – Honey

This week’s Video of the week slot goes to the hyper-productive King Gizzard & The Lizard Wizard and their depiction of Melbourne at nightfall.

We have to be honest here, we were a bit alienated by their previous releases Fishing for Fishies and Infest the Rats’ Nest. Yet, with this new video they seem to get back on the “right” path.

In the meanwhile, King Gizzard released another video Some of Us in which they reminds us of the problems the world had before the Covid-19 crisis. Despite the pandemics, humanity is still destined for extinction.

About

In the lost and found is a joint project of Elena Davcheva and Igor Stojanovski. The website is a public archive of our own writings about music as well as a platform for promoting new and exciting bands and artists.

We are mostly focused on independent and underground music. More specifically, we are into psychedelic rock and its derivatives, different forms of ambient and mainly experimental music. Yet, we do not shy away from more exposed music artists as well. No rules apply here and we are not trying to be snobs at all.
